Nusa Kambangan

Republic of Indonesia Raya

Tentang Wisata

Nusa Kambangan is an island southeast of Indonesia’s island Bali and a district of Klungkung Regency that includes the neighbouring small island of Nusa Lembongan. The Badung Strait separates the island and Bali. The interior of Nusa Kambangan is hilly with a maximum altitude of 524 metres. It is drier than the nearby island of Bali.
